Best Homemade Alfredo Sauce

Description:

Alfredo sauce is a rich and creamy delight, originating from the heart of Rome and cherished for its simple yet luxurious blend of ingredients. It primarily consists of butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, melted together into a smooth and velvety sauce that clings perfectly to pasta. The sauce's indulgent texture and mild, comforting flavor profile make it a popular choice for a variety of pasta dishes, particularly fettuccine Alfredo, where it gently coats each strand, enhancing without overpowering.

This sauce's beauty lies in its versatility. While traditional Alfredo is sublime in its simplicity, the sauce also serves as a splendid canvas for customization. Additions like garlic, herbs, chicken, or seafood can transform it into a hearty meal that satisfies the soul. Whether served as a weeknight dinner or part of a festive gathering, Alfredo sauce brings a touch of Italian elegance to the table, promising a culinary experience that is both decadent and deeply satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1 1/2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 Teaspoons Garlic Minced
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Pepper
  • 2 Cups Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. Add the butter and cream to a large skillet.
  2. Simmer over low heat for 2 minutes.
  3. Whisk in the garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper for one minute.
  4. Whisk in the parmesan cheese until melted.
  5. Serve immediately.
